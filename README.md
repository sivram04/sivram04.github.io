Passenger & Traffic Forecasting Project (2025–2030) Developed a predictive analytics system for the Port Authority of NY & NJ to forecast traffic flow across terminals and bridges. Engineered a SQL/Python pipeline to process 20 years of historical data, climate metrics, and passenger logs. Implemented Prophet and XGBoost models for time-series forecasting and used K-Means clustering for carrier optimization. Delivered interactive Power BI dashboards providing actionable insights for infrastructure planning and congestion management.
